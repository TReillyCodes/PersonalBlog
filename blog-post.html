Exploring the African Coast

Published on Sep 11, 2024 by Trevor

Hey everyone! As the end of the year gets closer, I'm super excited about an upcoming adventure: a spectacular journey along the African coast aboard the Norwegian Dawn. This November, I'll be setting sail from the vibrant city of Barcelona, Spain, and making my way south toward the legendary port of Cape Town, South Africa. I've been looking forward to this trip for months, and the itinerary promises a perfect mix of cultural exploration, breathtaking landscapes, and some much-needed relaxation on the open seas.

Starting in Barcelona

Our voyage kicks off in Barcelona, one of Europe's most iconic cities. I'll be spending a couple of days before departure exploring its stunning architecture, from the grandeur of Sagrada Família to the whimsical Park Güell. The city's unique blend of history and modernity always leaves me in awe, and it's the perfect place to set the tone for the journey ahead.

As the Norwegian Dawn pulls out of the Port of Barcelona, I’ll be surrounded by fellow travelers, all eager to embark on this incredible 21-day adventure. The ship itself is amazing, with endless amenities that will make the days at sea just as thrilling as the ports of call.

First Stop: Canary Islands

Our first destination? The Canary Islands! This volcanic archipelago is known for its otherworldly landscapes, pristine beaches, and unique charm. I'll be docking at Santa Cruz de Tenerife, where I plan to explore the island's lush rainforests, hike through ancient lava fields, and take in the incredible views from Mount Teide, Spain’s highest peak.

With its year-round sunshine, Tenerife offers the perfect respite before we head deeper into the southern Atlantic. I’m already dreaming of afternoons spent sipping local wine and evenings on the ship, watching the sun set over the horizon.

Africa Awaits

As we make our way down the African coastline, the journey becomes truly epic. Our next stops will include Dakar, Senegal, and Walvis Bay, Namibia. In Senegal, I’m looking forward to exploring the vibrant local markets, sampling West African cuisine, and learning about the rich cultural heritage of the region.

In Namibia, the surreal desert landscapes of Sossusvlei and the towering red sand dunes are calling my name. Walvis Bay is a hidden gem, with its rich marine life and the opportunity to witness seals, flamingos, and maybe even a dolphin or two while we sail along the coast.

The Grand Finale: Cape Town, South Africa

The highlight of the trip, and where the Norwegian Dawn journey will end, is Cape Town, South Africa. Nestled at the base of the mighty Table Mountain, Cape Town is one of the most stunning cities in the world. I can’t wait to take the cable car to the top of the mountain and soak in panoramic views of the city, the ocean, and the distant Robben Island.

From the vibrant V&A Waterfront to the historic Robben Island and the beautiful Cape Winelands, Cape Town offers something for everyone. I’m especially excited to spend some time in Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens, and hopefully, get a chance to try some shark diving off the coast of Gansbaai—a true adrenaline rush to end the trip on a high note.

Looking Forward

Trips like these fuel my passion for exploring new destinations and sharing my experiences with all of you. This 21-day cruise along the African coast will offer a perfect blend of relaxation, adventure, and discovery. I’m beyond excited to set sail and document every moment of this unforgettable journey. Stay tuned for updates as I navigate my way from Barcelona to Cape Town—I’ll be posting my favorite spots, hidden gems, and travel tips along the way!
